About Me

Nicole Frazier, is a junior web developer from Colorado Springs, Colorado. She completed a dual Bachelor's of Arts in English and Spanish at Colorado State University. During and undergraduate course called Writing for the Web, she learned basic HTML and CSS skills and built websites from scratch, and developed an interest in technology. From July 2017 to January 2018, she enrolled in a Code Bootcamp at the University of Denver. Nicole loves to design and build things and also is passionate about global flora and fauna conservation efforts.
